Engineering with Heart and Expertise

Bringing affordable, bankable energy to Eswatini and South Africa.

Born from a Need

Founded in December 2024 by Thulani Ngwenya, our company was born from a direct struggle. Witnessing the daily load reduction and load shedding in Matsafeni, Thulani was motivated to target underprivileged societies with reliable electrical solutions.

With 5 years of experience at Solsun as a designer, installer, and maintainer, and a strong academic background in electrical engineering, Thulani bridges the gap between technical excellence and community care.

"We treat every customer like a family member, caring enough to bring only the best equipment."
